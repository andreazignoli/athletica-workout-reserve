What is it?

The Athletica Workout Reserve allows you to track your performance relative to your maximum.

  • 100%: It means you are fresh and at the start of your activity.
  • 0%: It means you hit a personal best.

In the example below, the Garmin watch indicates that still 22.9% remain until you reach your best performance.

How it works?

  • Invisible monitoring: Athletica builds your power/speed profile.
  • Seamless Integration: Works with Garmin devices and integrates directly with Garmin Connect for a smooth experience.
  • Optimized for Athletes: Built with sports performance optimization in mind, perfect for trainers, athletes, and coaches.
