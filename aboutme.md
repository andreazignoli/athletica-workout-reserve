The **Athletica Workout Reserve** is a tool designed to quantify an athlete's remaining margin relative to their historical performance. This innovative approach integrates complex physiological factors into a single, user-friendly metric, enabling athletes and coaches to optimize training prescriptions for enhanced performance.

---

## What is Workout Reserve?

The Workout Reserve provides a **real-time performance metric** that answers a critical question during training: *"How much do I have left in the tank?"*

- **100%** means you're fresh and at the start of your activity
- **0%** means you've reached a personal best performance for that duration

By analyzing your past performance data, Workout Reserve provides real-time insights into how much effort you can sustain, facilitating more informed training decisions and better management of training loads.

---

## How It Works

### Invisible Monitoring

Athletica continuously builds your power/speed profile from your training data, creating a personalized performance baseline across different durations and intensities.

### Real-Time Calculation

During your workout, the Workout Reserve metric updates in real-time on your Garmin device, showing your remaining capacity relative to your best performances.

### AI-Powered Updates

Seamlessly integrates with Athletica's AI engine to keep your athlete profile up-to-date, ensuring accurate and personalized metrics.
